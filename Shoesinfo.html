LOAFERS

loafers are kind of the perfect pair of classic shoes. Reminiscent of a moccasin with leather uppers and a flat heel, this slip-on shoe is versatile, cool, and classic all at the same time. Loafers sit right on the edge between casual and formal mens shoes, which means you can wear them to the office, on a date, or out running errands.
